Description: When two loops are nested, swaps the loops headers.

Example:

  • 1. Select the desired for loop to be changed and choose Interchange under the Refactoring menu.
  • 2. Next you will be prompted for the depth of the loop to be switched.
  • 3. The resulting for loop.

Use:

Restrictions:

  • 1. The selected loops must be perfectly nested.
